I am an AI Architect at Typeface, working on multimodal AI and generative content systems.

Previously, I co-founded TensorTour Inc. (acquired by Typeface, 2024), where we built visual AI products. I hold a Ph.D. from IIT Delhi.

Before that, I spent 11 years at C-DAC building national-scale digital infrastructure serving ~500 million citizens, including e-RaktKosh, National Voter Service Portal, Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission, API Setu, UMANG, and e-Sushrut.
